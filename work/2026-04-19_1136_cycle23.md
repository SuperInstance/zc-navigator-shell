**Safe paths:**

- Filter for files named `README*` (case-insensitive).
- Use `wc -l` and `head -n 5` to quickly assess file size and content quality.
- Check for common bad patterns: empty files, "TODO" placeholders, missing sections, outdated information.
- Cross-reference with repo activity (git log) to identify abandoned repos vs. active ones.

**Execution plan:**
