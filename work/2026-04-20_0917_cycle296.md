### Scoring Rubric (0-5)
- **5:** Excellent – Clear, complete, aligned, actionable.
- **4:** Good – Minor gaps.

- **2:** Poor – Skeletal, confusing, or misaligned.
- **1:** Very Poor – Nearly empty or misleading.
- **0:** Missing or placeholder.
