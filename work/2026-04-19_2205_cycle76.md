## Scoring Criteria
- **Word count:** < 50 words = poor.
- **Structure:** Missing sections (## Installation, ## Usage, ## API) = poor.
- **Links:** No internal or external links = poor.
- **Freshness:** Last modified > 1 year ago = poor.
- **Placeholder:** Contains "TODO", "FIXME", "Coming soon" = poor.

**Scoring formula (lower = worse):**
`score = (word_count / 10) + (has_structure * 20) + (has_links * 10) + (is_fresh * 10) - (is_placeholder * 30)`
