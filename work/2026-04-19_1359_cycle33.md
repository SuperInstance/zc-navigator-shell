### Step 1 — Map the negative space (P0)
**Rocks:**
- Don't assume all repos are under `/home` — some may be in `/mnt`, `/opt`, or other mounts.
- Don't rely on file extensions alone; READMEs can be `.md`, `.txt`, `.rst`, or have no extension.
- Don't modify files during scan phase; analysis only.
- Don't include repos that are intentionally minimal (e.g., empty template repos).
- Don't trust `ls -la` output for large directories; use `find` with limits.

### Step 2 — Identify safe channels (P1)
**Safe Paths:**
- Use `find` command starting from known fleet root directories.
- Filter for files named `README*` (case-insensitive).
- Read first 10 lines of each file to assess quality.
- Score based on: presence of description, installation, usage, and links.
- Sort by score ascending to find worst.
