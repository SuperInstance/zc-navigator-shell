### **C. Evaluation of Available READMEs & Methodology**

**Scoring Criteria (0-5, where 0 is worst):**
-   **5 (Exemplary):** Clear purpose, setup instructions, usage examples, API docs, contribution guidelines.
-   **4 (Good):** Missing one minor element (e.g., contribution guidelines).
-   **3 (Adequate):** Core purpose and setup are clear, but lacks examples or detailed docs.
-   **2 (Poor):** Unclear purpose, incomplete or missing setup, major gaps.
-   **1 (Very Poor):** Mostly placeholder text, severely outdated, or misleading.
-   **0 (Nonexistent/Placeholder):** No README, or only a title/one line.
