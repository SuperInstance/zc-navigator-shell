## README Scoring Rubric (0-10, 10=best)
- **Existence (0/2):** 0=No README, 1=Placeholder/empty, 2=File exists with content.
- **Structure (0/3):** 0=No headers, 1=Basic title, 2=Sections, 3=Clear sections (Overview, Install, Use).
- **Clarity (0/3):** 0=Unintelligible, 1=Vague, 2=Understandable, 3=Clear and concise.
- **Completeness (0/2):** 0=Missing critical info, 1=Partial info, 2=Has installation, usage, and purpose.
